FAQ Section

1. What is Gurunath Getaways ?

Gurunath Getaways is an initiative started by a few students of College Of Engineering , Guindy to promote adventure and tourism and most importantly spark the minds of people to explore a plethora of destinations all around the world !

2. Why choose this ?

GG. Well , People who visit this site do exclaim this , as Gurunath Getaways offers a different outlook towards a city's tourism and cultural point of view. The motto of this is to inspire , and make people dream to reach their destinations !

3. Can intra-city transport be arranged ?

Yes ! Even though this website offers more of a brochure-like experience , you can reach out to us by sending us a message as to what your requirements are (Contact Us) , and we'll get things sorted ;)
