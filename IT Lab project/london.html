London, capital of England,is one of the most important tourist placees in the world
Standing on river Thames, London is one artistically designed and is a highly developed city.

The main attractions of London include the Buckingham Palace, The Big Ben, and the London Eye. Potterheads must definitely go and visit the Warner Bros Harry Potter Studio Tour which contains all the setwork and artefacts used for the Harry Potter movies. With football everywheere in the air, football fans have hell lotta iconic places to visit like Wembley Stadium, Stamford Bridge and the Emirates. Not to forget, the night view from The Shard gives us a beautiful top view of the entire city.
